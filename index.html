The experience of making the document made me feel more motivated and determined in learning more skills in MEDPL 150. During this course, I’m hoping to learn and gain basic and complex software skills such as all the Adobe apps and other software websites in editing my own portfolio. I am mostly hoping to gain software editing skills and coding that can help me with media software art such as illustration and graphic design.
